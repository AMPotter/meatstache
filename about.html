About Us

Here at Meatstache, quality meats and quality mustaches are our absolute top priorities. We dream of a world populated by well-groomed mustaches dripping with the juices of a locally grass-fed steak. We pride ourselves on sourcing only from the smallest and most organic of local farms. Our beef steaks are all grass-finished, and all other beef and pork products are grass-fed. Our sausages are made fresh, daily, in-house by our dedicated team of stern men with facial hair. Our mustache products are made from all-natural oils and waxes, and are never animal-tested. Rather, we test them on ourselves, and we all look amazing, always.
